Georgian Scooters National Federation

    Georgian Scooters National Federation is membership based non-commercial legal entity. It was created for development of scooters industry in Georgia which includes: Scooters,Electric Scooters,Snow scooters.Another reason of its creation is reducing of air pollution in Georgia with the development of eco-friendly transport – scooters.

Federation membership

Federation membership is open to users of scooters, electric scooters and snow scooters, also to doctors and other working personal of scooter’s industry. Apart from it, members can be sport clubs and other legal entities’ engaged with scooters.

Federation is responsible for protection of its membership’s rights, also it is responsible for representing its members with government structures and international organizations.

Federation is currently open for new memberships. Membership enlistment is secured by federation board.
