25 February, 2022

With the organization of Georgian Scooters National Federation on 25th of February at Akaki Tsereteli State University in Kutaisi mutual cooperation coordinating meeting was held with Kutaisi City Hall.

Meeting participants were : Kutaisi Vice mayor David Eremeishvili, City Hall head of administrative services Petre Adamadze, city hall Economic Development and Local Government Property and Transport Service head Mirza Mamasakhlisi. Apart from it meeting was attended by Imereti Olympic Committee Chairman Revaz Robakidze and Kutaisi Majoritarian MP bureau representatives.

Also International center “Eurobridge” founders and Kutaisi International University (KIU) employees were present at the event. What is more important event was attended by representatives of scooters industry in Georgia : NIU E-Scooters, Kicky.city, Xiaomi Georgia , Metromart , IYI Electric Scooter

Our federation have presented report of its one year activates at the event. Also discussed was the perspectives of development of the electric scooter infrastructure in Imereti Region and the evaluation of eco-friendly projects support. As a result next coordinating meeting was planned where most actual infrastructure concrete cases will be presented by sides. Also was planned participation in 2 May Event “Kutaisoba” for the increase of the awareness towards ecofriendly transport.

We thank Kutaisi City Hall, parliament bureau and all representatives of scooter industries for the presence. Also we thank International Center “Eurobridge” for the support in organizing meeting.
