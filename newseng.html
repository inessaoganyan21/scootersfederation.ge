April 3,2021

On 3rd of April organized by Georgian National Scooters Federation special seminar / meeting was held with citizenship activist platform, subject of the meeting was “Healthy alternative opportunities in Tbilisi transport industry”. Following questions were discussed on the meeting:

  • Air pollution indexes and their effect
  • Age of Tbilisi auto park and technical service procedure changes
  • Development of Bicycle & Scooter road infrastructure in Tbilisi
  • Enhancing citizens awareness via citizenship activism

Thanks to the citizenship activist platform for participation and for their new ideas. Such meeting will be held in future too with more scale.

April 10, 2021

On 10th of April Georgian Scooters National Federation together with civil activists implemented its first field civil activation by cleaning the environment in Tbilisi at Turtle Lake territory.

As a result of the activation Turtle Lake walking trails and resting forest-park locations were completely cleaned up. Apart from it federation representatives had small talks with Turtle Lake visitors emphasizing the need to protect the environment.

Such on field events will take place in future too since environmental protection and enhancing civil awareness on this matter is one of the priorities for our federation.

Let's take care of the environment together.
